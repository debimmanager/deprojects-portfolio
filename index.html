About Me

I’m a forward-thinking and results-driven BIM Manager with over 9 years of experience transforming how engineering teams approach design, coordination, and construction. My professional journey has been rooted in integrating advanced Building Information Modeling (BIM) methodologies with emerging technologies, particularly Artificial Intelligence (AI), to enhance precision, collaboration, and speed across the project lifecycle.

I specialize in leading multidisciplinary teams through the digital transformation process—from traditional CAD workflows to fully integrated BIM environments that support design automation, prefabrication, and intelligent data exchange. I’ve developed and implemented scalable BIM strategies for complex projects, particularly in the MEP and mission-critical data center sectors, ensuring that models are not just visually accurate but also data-rich, construction-ready, and lifecycle-driven.

My work is deeply focused on operational efficiency. I actively explore the convergence of BIM and AI—using tools like PyRevit, Revit Python Shell, Dynamo, and AI-powered copilots—to automate repetitive tasks, identify design conflicts early, generate insights from BIM data, and empower teams to focus on higher-value decisions. I believe in building digital ecosystems where models are not just 3D visuals, but living databases that support design intelligence, facilities management, and strategic project delivery.
